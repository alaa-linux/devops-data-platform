# Grafana Alerts Configuration

## Objectif

Configurer les alertes Grafana pour le monitoring KPI.

---

## Alertes prévues

### anomalies
- Warning > 10
- Critical > 20

### duplicate_rows
- Warning > 50
- Critical > 100

### total_nulls
- Warning > 0
- Critical > 5

### ingestion_rows
- Critical < 100

---

## Canaux de notification

- Email
- Slack
- Webhook
- Microsoft Teams

---

## Dashboards concernés

- Data Quality Dashboard
- Real-Time Streaming Dashboard

---

## Statut

Configuration Grafana préparée.
